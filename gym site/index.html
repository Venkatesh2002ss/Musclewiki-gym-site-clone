About MuscleWiki

Started in 2022 to help newbies in the gym, MuscleWiki
has grown to be the largest database of exercises in the
world. Using the expertise of our Chief Muscle Officer-
Coach Ty - to curate a comprehensive resource of
exercises, workouts and routines.

We are on a mission to create the most accessible and
useful content to help not only beginners, but even the
most battle hardened gym goer with their form and
reaching their goals. MuscleWiki aims to be the best way
to simplify your workout.
